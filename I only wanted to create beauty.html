rebel without a cause

I just wanted to create beauty.

12-10-2024

The most joy I get from life is when creating. When I’m all alone divorced of all life’s troubles solely focused on my craft. My happiest moments in life (aside from the moments when I was near her) were when I was in a flow state, not feeling like I’m human and just letting whatever the universe was feeling flow through me.

I loved drawing and painting even though I sucked at it. Actually, right now while I’m writing I am feeling a little bit happy. I loved creating robots and learning. God, I love learning so much.

The thing is that I didn’t ask for all of this. I hate people so much. I hate meeting someone and trying my best to detect whether they are truthful or not. Hypocrisy man, lot of selfish, dumb, judgemental, hypocrites exist nowadays. Like I hate that I have to ask myself if what I’m saying right now to a person will probably get taken out of context and get terribly paraphrased and told to someone. This is neither human nor healthy. The biggest problem of them all is that I am 18 right now. And if I’m meeting those kinds of people today, what kind of horrible twisted people I’ll meet tomorrow. It just makes me hate humanity more and more everyday.

I really hate that my image to people is that I am a crazy nerd to people. It’s sounds corny I know but for some reason I come off as that to everyone even though I try to act and dress as normal as I can.

I’m so tired of people. I think I am going to quit everything in two days.

I genuinely I believe I am meant to create and to create only. I am not meant to have human relationships whether platonic or not. All I have been doing the past 18 years aside from sharpening skills was a mistake, including all the relationships I had. I just wish I had the guts to cut off everything that doesn’t involve designing. The problem is I just know people will come up with the dumbest reasons for what I’m doing, and I hate when people talk about me.

I just want to read, learn, and build all day.
